Why we call it “Aurix”

Aurix comes from Auris (Latin for “ear” and “hearing”) plus X (technology, the unknown, and what comes next). Together it means “hearing power / an acoustic core”—a name that feels short, clean, and unapologetically tech. We wanted something that, in one second, makes people think of hearing, sound waves, and even a chip-like system working quietly in the background. That’s the point: Aurix shouldn’t feel like a “medical label.” It should feel like smart everyday audio—so low-income communities can wear it with confidence, get clearer speech in noise, and stay safer in real-world environments. The midnight-black base with a blue/purple glow reinforces that feeling: modern, discreet, and “smart tech”, not clinical.
